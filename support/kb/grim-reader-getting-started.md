## Connecting to your server

1. Open the app and enter the address of your **Grimmory** server (for example
   `https://books.home.net`).
2. Sign in with your username and password.
3. Your libraries appear automatically.

## Reading a book

Tap a book and choose **Download**. After that you can read it offline — EPUBs in
a full-screen paged reader, and PDF, comics and audiobooks are supported too.

## No connection?

Check that your server is reachable from your phone and that the address starts
with `https://`. Still stuck? [Let us know](/support/).
